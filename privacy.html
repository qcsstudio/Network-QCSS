Privacy draft

Consent-aware tracking and lead capture policy.

This is a starter policy for the MVP. Before production launch, review it with a qualified legal/privacy advisor for your target countries.

What We Collect

We may collect contact details you submit, assessment answers, service interest, page interactions, campaign attribution, approximate country, device/browser information, and server request metadata such as IP address.

How Consent Works

Necessary storage is used for security and basic operation. Analytics, marketing, and personalization tracking should run only after you choose those options in the cookie banner.

How We Use Data

We use data to respond to inquiries, generate assessment results, qualify leads, improve content, measure campaigns, and send relevant follow-up where consent allows.

Email and Identity

The website cannot read your email from your browser. We receive email or phone information only when you submit a form, book a call, download a gated resource, or contact us directly.

Your Choices

You can decline optional cookies, unsubscribe from marketing messages, or request correction/deletion of your contact information.
